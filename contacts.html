About

Architecture and research enthusiast specializing in 3D reconstruction of point-cloud environments from 360° images.

I use street-view imagery (SVI) and both 2D and 3D data to analyze urban environments — from panoramic street-level capture to post-reconstruction spatial pipelines. I am pursuing a Master of Architecture at Southeast University with a research focus in urban studies.

Research interests: street-view urban analytics · 3D reconstruction from 360° imagery · pedestrian exposure · structure-from-motion postprocessing

Education

  • Bachelor of Arts in Architecture — American University of Phnom Penh, Cambodia (2019–2024)
