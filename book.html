The Beauty of Japan Book

A picture book capturing architecture, landscape, and everyday scenes across Japan — curated as a visual journal of place and light.

The Beauty of Japan collects photographs and short notes from travels and studies across Japanese cities and countryside. Each spread pairs image and context — shrines, streets, seasonal light, and urban texture.
